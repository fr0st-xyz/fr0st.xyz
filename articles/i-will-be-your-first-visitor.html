I'll be your first visitor

Views:



Since I like spending time on websites and reading what people put online, I thought I would make an article about being your first visitor, because I genuinely think it is a good idea.

I know everyone has stories to tell, but even if you do not have a story and you just have a website, that is still good, and it does not have to be perfect or finished. If you have something online, you can send it to me so I can read it or just check out your website. I'd really like to see it.

I am kind of already doing this anyway. Right now, I have a list of websites that I read sometimes, saved in my notes and stuff, and it would be nice if yours ended up there too. I would genuinely love that.

So yeah, if you have a blog or a website, just send it. Do not overthink it and do not be shy. I will read it. I will be your first visitor literally.

If you want to send something, you can reach me at yo@fr0st.xyz or Discord.
